Club Updates

Feb 22, 2025

Hey everyone!

Quick updates from the CAIAC team:

  • We’re having another social this Friday at 1:30 PM in the basement of Avery Library. RSVP here.
  • The first two weeks of the fellowship programs have been great! Thank you to all the facilitators and participants. We created an anonymous feedback form if anyone wants to share ideas/feedback/criticism to help improve the program (or CAIAC in general). I’d also like to share our technical career guide and policy career guide for those of you looking for professional experience in AI safety. Reminder that the fellowship curriculums are public (technical and policy) in case you want to self-study or you miss a meeting.
  • One of the central goals of this club is to catalyze research projects in AI safety. We want to enable you guys to do work in both technical AIS and policy. To kick start CAIAC’s research efforts we’ve opened applications for our Research Groups program. See the application form for more details.
  • In our quest to expand the club’s activities, we’re looking for members who want to take on leadership roles. Specifically we’re looking for a Research Groups Lead, an Operations Lead, and an Events Lead, but we’re open to other possibilities if you’re particularly excited about contributing to CAIAC. We’re looking for self-starters who are highly engaged with AI safety and are willing to commit a minimum of 5 hours/week to CAIAC-related administrative work. Apply here. Underclassmen are especially encouraged to apply.
  • Finally, we’re making use of the CAIAC Slack to share AI safety related resources, opportunities, and anything else. Join the conversation!

Best,
